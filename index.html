Michael's Biography Website

Michael Buckwalter is a charismatic, responsible, and disciplined individual. People who don’t know Michael might say he is uncaring about many things, but this couldn’t be further from the truth. He is assertive, decisive, and determined to be successful. To most, Michael is a student and co-worker, to his loved ones he is a brother and a friend.

Michael attends Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University and is majoring in Accounting and Finance. On the outside, Michael might seem like a typical college student who is just there to do the bare minimum and sacrifice their education for fun, but this is not the case. Michael goes above and beyond in his academic career. With a 3.93 overall college GPA, Michael is the student professors generally recommend to the board heads of scholarships and academically competitive extracurricular activities. His academic merit has earned him a spot on the BASIS team at Virginia Tech, in which students manage a five million dollar bond portfolio for the Virginia Tech Foundation.

Michael is a part-time employee at Target. He is experienced in order fulfillment, checkout advocacy, service advocacy, and grocery. Michael is a responsible employee and he has a great work ethic. He doesn't like to stand around when there are tasks to be done. Michael is active in the workplace and his leaders take recognition of that. Michael is usually one of the first team members a leader will ask to do something. Michael has been known to pull the Bullseye one-for-ones, push products to the floor, and jump in anywhere the team needs a hand when he is asked to.

Michael is a member of BASIS (Bonds and Securities Investing by Students) at Virginia Tech. Apart from managing 5 million dollars, this entails fancy events, presentations, and getting out into the community. One such community event that BASIS partakes in is The Big Event hosted by Tech. This is the second largest volunteering event in the nation! Here you can see Michael and a fellow BASIS member volunteering.
